Our Plan

Our website is meant to make volunteering easier for high schoolers, adults and really anyone looking for a nice way to spend their time!

Our Story

We’re Christina, Katherine, and Maanasa from the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at the Adobe San Jose location! We spent five weeks learning programming languages and computer basics to prepare us to spend the last two weeks completing a final project. This program has been an amazing experience; a fact helped by our fellow friends in the class, our fantastic teacher and TAs and of course our love for coding! We are girls who code now and in the future.

About Us

Maanasa Kotha

She is currently a senior at San Jose High. Maanasa absolutely loves all things Harry Potter and is pretty much obsessed with superhero anything. She is also extremely passionate about the diversity issues in STEAM fields and hopes to end the gender and ethnicity gap in STEAM.
