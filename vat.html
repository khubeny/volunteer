About Us

Who are we? We are a close-knit group of weirdos who love to code and be the strangest and most creative we can be. We were brought together through the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program and together we developed this project in order to help others find volunteer opportunities. This is extremely helpful for teens looking for volunteer hours, adults with free time and a desire to contribute to the community and really all who want something to do that will benefit the people around them.
